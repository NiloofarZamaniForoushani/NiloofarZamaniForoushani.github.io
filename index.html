About Me

I am a PhD researcher at Imperial College Business School specializing in Operations & Analytics. My research interests focus on information design, pricing strategies in digital platforms, and developing interpretable stratification strategies for healthcare screening guidelines.

With a strong background in industrial engineering and data-driven decision making, I combine quantitative modeling and machine learning techniques to solve complex real-world problems. My expertise spans econometrics, stochastic optimization, and business analytics.

I am passionate about translating research into practical applications and have experience working with industry partners to implement data-driven solutions that drive measurable improvements in efficiency and performance.
